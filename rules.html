Grade 12 Athena Rules

1. Arrive on time and be prepared.
2. Respect teachers, classmates, and property.
3. Listen actively when others speak.
4. Raise your hand to speak or ask questions.
5. Keep the classroom clean and organized.
6. Follow instructions the first time given.
7. No food, drinks, or gum in class (water may be allowed).
8. Use electronic devices only for learning.
9. Participate positively in all activities.
10. Take responsibility for your actions and work.
